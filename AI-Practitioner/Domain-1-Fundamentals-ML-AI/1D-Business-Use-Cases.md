# Deep Dive

## Applications where AI Provides Value
### Assisting Human Decision-Making
  * AI provides insights for better human decisions.
  * **Examples from Industry:**
    * FINRA Enhances Market Surveillance (Uses AWS ML to analyze 75 billion market events daily).
    * Siemens Optimizes Energy Consumption (Uses AWS ML to analyze energy consumption use to optimize building energy use).
    * Cerner Improves Patient Care (Uses AWS ML to predict patient health risks, supporting proactive healthcare decisions).
    * Yelp Enhances Recommendation Systems (Use Amazon SageMaker to offer personalized recommendations, enhances user descision-making). 
### Solution Scalability
  * AI models can handle large data and diverse use cases.
  * **Examples from Industry:**
    * AirBNB Handles Peak Demands (Scales with AWS to handle demand and AWS ML to match guests with hosts efficiently).
    * ZocDoc Scales Appointment Booking (Uses AWS to scale appointment booking, matching patients with healthcare providers as the user base grows).
    * Duolingo Expands Language Learning (Employs AWS to personalize language lessons, accommodating millions of users worldwide).
    * Expedia Scales Travel Booking Services (Leverages AWS for scalable travel booking, personalized recommendations in real-time). 
### Automation
  * Steamlines repetitive tasks, improving efficiency and cutting costs.
  * **Examples from Industry:**
    * Schneider Electric Automates Energy Management (Automates energy management in buildings using AI).
    * Toyota Enhances Vehicle Production (Uses AWS ML to automatic QC in production line via computer vision).
    * Intuit Automates Customer Support with Chatbots (Employs Amazon Lex chatbots to automate customer support, reduces wait times).
    * Sony DADC Automates Media Processing (Automates digital media processing with AWS ML, manages metadata and QC).
### Common Themes
  * AI improves comptetive position and customer focus.
  * AI reduces human errors.
  * AI increases productivity.

## Aligning AI with Business Goals
### Questions to Consider
  * What are the **top use cases** for my industry?
  * What is the **payoff**?
  * **How long** will it take to implement?
  * Do I have the **right resources**?
  * Do I have the **right data**?
  * Is it **legal**?
### Best Workflows for AI
  * High-value workflows where **complex decisions** are made largely on **past experience** or **intuition**.
  * **Inferences** can be used to provide a **better decision**.
### Business Metrics
  * **Cost per User:**
    * Calculate the average expense incurred to serve each individual user/customer.
  * **Development Costs:**
    * Select the right resources to minimize costs during development and operation.
    * Reduce *development costs* while maintaining performance. 
  * **Return on Investment (ROI):**
    * Measure both costs and business value delivered by the workloads.
    * Assess the profitability of different products and services.
  * **Customer Feedback:**
    * Insights, opinions, experiences shared by customer regarding products, services, and overall brand.
    * Collect through various channels (surveys, reviews, customer support tickers, social media, direct communication).
